The Project
The project is based on constructing a web portal for registering any complaints or problems from our region which is mainly solved by the government bodies and agencies. If we have any problem that the government requires to deal with them. Thus, we can search in the portal for the problem and the portal will recommend us with the concerned divisions to whom we should approach. It will also have us updated with the solved issues from our region. If the problem is not dealt with the charge can be sent to the higher authorities of the respective divisions. after they deal with the problem an individual can provide evaluation and ratings of the job performed by the officials.
